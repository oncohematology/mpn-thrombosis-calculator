MPN Thrombosis Residual Risk Score v0.3 preliminary

Thrombotic risk stratification in PV and ET patients without prior thrombosis
⚠ For research purposes only. This calculator implements a draft score derived from systematic literature review. It has not undergone prospective clinical validation and is not approved as a medical device. Do not use as a substitute for clinical judgement or for individual treatment decisions.

Scope: This score applies only to PV and ET patients without any prior thrombotic event. Patients with a history of thrombosis are classified high-risk by ELN/IPSET-t consensus and receive cytoreduction regardless of this score.

Before starting: eligibility check

The present score was derived for and validated in the population of patients without prior thrombotic events, where current guideline-based stratification is least granular and where the decision to initiate cytoreduction is most uncertain.

A separate score for stratifying recurrence risk in patients with prior thrombosis is under development as a follow-up investigation.
